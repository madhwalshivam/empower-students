EMPOWER STUDENTS — SPECIALIST PHOTO UPLOADS
============================================

Drop the photos into this folder. Recommended size: 600 × 600 px square,
under 200 KB each. JPG or WebP both fine.

Default filenames the seeded panel expects (you can change them from
/admin/  → Specialists section if you prefer different names):

    ot.jpg              — Senior Occupational Therapist
    speech.jpg          — Speech & Language Therapist
    psychologist.jpg    — Child Psychologist
    neurologist.jpg     — Paediatric Neurologist
    paeds.jpg           — Paediatrician
    counsellor.jpg      — Family Counsellor
    special_ed.jpg      — Special Educator

Optional brand assets:
    logo.png            — top-left site logo (transparent background)
    hero.jpg            — homepage hero image (1600 × 900 px)
    about.jpg           — about-page banner

Tip: WebP gives 30-50% smaller files at the same quality. If you upload
WebP, edit the filename in /admin/  → Specialists.
